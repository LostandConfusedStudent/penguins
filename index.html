Penguins

Here are some details:

Penguins are birds that cannot fly. Most species live in Antarcica, including:

  • Emperor
  • Chinstrap
  • Gentoo

They usually move around by waddling and hopping. They can also slide on their bellies.

Of course, it is very cold in antarctica and these little guys will need to be able to defend themselves. They huddle together to protect themselves from predators and the cold.
